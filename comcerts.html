Comecerts - the name stands for Concerts to come to - started in 2015 as a Munich concert series for German-Asian indie music and was created by the two students Adam Langer and Jonas Haesner.

In the meantime, in addition to the concert series, an agency has also emerged, which operates throughout Germany is responsible for concerts of bands, especially from the Asian region. Comecerts sees as a cultural connector in terms of indie pop and electronic music.

The purpose of the project was to develop a framework visuality that addresses the design needs as comprehensively as possible in terms of media. The aim is to develop a trend-setting corporate design for the music and cultural scene.
