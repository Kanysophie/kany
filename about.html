ABOUT

My name is Sophie Kany, I’m 24 years old and studying in the 3rd semester communication design at the UAS Munich. Since the first semester, I have been working intensively on typography and 3D graphic design. I always work conceptually and have great interest in branding and editorial projects.

In 2018 I completed my interior design studies. I am fascinated by the interdisciplinary work in both areas.

I have already been able to practice as part of my first studies in the office of architect Manuel Benjamin Schachtner.
